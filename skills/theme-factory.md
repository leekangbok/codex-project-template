# Theme Factory

## Purpose
Apply consistent, professional styling to artifacts (slides, documents, web pages).

## Available Themes
1.  **Ocean Depths**: Maritime blues and professional grays.
2.  **Sunset Boulevard**: Warm oranges and vibrant reds.
3.  **Forest Canopy**: Grounded greens and earth tones.
4.  **Modern Minimalist**: Grayscale with high contrast.
5.  **Golden Hour**: Rich autumnal golds and ambers.
6.  **Arctic Frost**: Cool crisps blues and whites.
7.  **Desert Rose**: Sophisticated pinks and mauves.
8.  **Tech Innovation**: Bold blacks and electric accents.
9.  **Botanical Garden**: Fresh organic greens and florals.
10. **Midnight Galaxy**: Dramatic deep purples and space blacks.

## Usage
- **Review**: Show the user available themes.
- **Apply**: Use the theme's color palette and font pairings consistently across the artifact.
- **Custom Theme**: If none of the 10 themes fit, generate a new theme on-the-fly with a descriptive name and harmonious colors/fonts.

## Brand Consistency
- Ensure high readability and contrast.
- Maintain the visual identity across all pages or components.
- Default to professional font pairings (e.g., a bold serif for headers and a clean sans-serif for body).
